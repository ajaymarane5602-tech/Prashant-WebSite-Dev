Our Journey

Vandika Harvest started in Sangli with one mission: deliver haldi-first products that stay fresh till the last spoonful. What began with turmeric now includes curated kitchen essentials trusted by homes and small food businesses.

  1. 2021

    Started with farm-direct turmeric sourcing and controlled sun-drying.

  2. 2023

    Expanded into premium haldi variations and a broader essentials line.

  3. 2026

    Strengthened small-batch operations with tighter consistency and packaging upgrades.
